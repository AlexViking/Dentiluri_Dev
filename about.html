About Dentiluri

Providing exceptional dental care to our community for over 20 years.

Our Story

Dentiluri was founded in 2003 by Dr. Sarah Johnson with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, compassionate dental care to patients of all ages. What started as a small practice has grown into a trusted dental care provider in our community.

Our team of experienced dentists, hygienists, and staff are dedicated to making your dental experience as comfortable and stress-free as possible. We believe in building long-term relationships with our patients based on trust, respect, and exceptional care.

At Dentiluri, we stay at the forefront of dental technology and techniques to ensure you receive the most effective and comfortable treatments available.
